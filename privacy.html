  • Right to Lodge Complaints: Contact your local data protection authority
  • Right to Data Protection Officer: Contact our DPO at privacy@smartfinancehub.vip
  • Right to Cross-Border Transfers: Information about international data transfers

Data Transfers

When we transfer your data outside the EU, we ensure adequate protection through:

Under GDPR, we obtain explicit consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. You can manage your preferences through our cookie banner.

11. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

Categories of Personal Information We Collect

  • Identifiers: Email addresses, IP addresses, cookie IDs
  • Internet Activity: Browsing history, page views, clicks
  • Geolocation Data: General location information
  • Inferences: User preferences and interests derived from activity

California Privacy Rights

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to Correct: Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices
  • Right to Limit: Limit the use of sensitive personal information

Sale and Sharing of Personal Information

We do not "sell" personal information in the traditional sense. However, under CCPA's broad definition, sharing data with advertising partners may be considered a "sale."

  • Advertising Partners: Google AdSense and other ad networks
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics
  • Opt-Out Method: Use our "Do Not Sell My Information" link

Exercising California Rights

California residents can exercise their rights by:

  • Email: privacy@smartfinancehub.vip
  • Phone: 1-800-PRIVACY (1-800-774-8229)
  • Online Form: Available on our website

Do Not Sell My Personal Information: Click here to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information.

12. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: Software and security patches applied promptly
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats and vulnerabilities
  • Backup Security: Encrypted backups with restricted access
  • Staff Training: Regular security awareness training for all team members

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Immediately investigate and contain the breach
  • Notify affected users within 72 hours (where required by law)
  • Notify relevant supervisory authorities as required
  • Provide clear information about what happened and what we're doing
  • Implement additional safeguards to prevent future incidents

Third-Party Security

We carefully vet all third-party service providers and require them to maintain appropriate security standards through contractual agreements.

13. Children's Privacy

Smart Finance Hub is designed for adults and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in the EU).

Age Verification

  • We do not request age information during newsletter signup
  • Our content is specifically targeted at adult audiences
  • We rely on parental supervision for younger users who may access our content

If We Learn of Child Data Collection

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under the applicable age, we will:

  • Delete the information immediately

  • Notify parents/guardians if required by law

Parental Rights

Parents or guardians who believe we may have collected information from their child should contact us immediately at privacy@smartfinancehub.vip.
